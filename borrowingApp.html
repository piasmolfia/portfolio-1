Borrowing Clothes Mobile App

Overview

Whether you buy or rent your clothes, the bill can rack up. Is it worth it to buy a shirt you will only wear once?
About the app:
Created a mobile "app" for an interactive design class that allows people to borrow clothes from users near them. Over 7 weeks, I worked collaboritvty in a team of 3 to create this app.
Solution:

Created an app that allows users to borrow clothes from others. This allows users to complete their temporary look they had in mind without spendind money.
